About

MM Logic Games is a small studio focused on classic logic puzzles, reimagined with a calm, paper-and-ink aesthetic. We make games for people who want their phones to feel a little quieter.

Founded and run by Miljko Milosavljević. Based in Serbia. Available worldwide.
